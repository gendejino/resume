About the Hilton Academy

Spanning 200 square miles, Hilton Academy serves the educational needs of Melbourne, Australia. Located in one of the state's fastest growing areas, the district enjoys a diversified economic base. Currently, the district consists of Undergraduate and Graduate programs. Hilton Academy is a comprehensive colleges. enrolling 1,250 students in 1st year up to graduating years. The school opened in the fall of 1987 and graduated its first academy-college in the spring of 1989. Hilton Academy is accredited by the Central Association of Colleges University and holds membership in the College Board and the National Association for College Admission Counseling
